@AGENTS.md
 ## Design Guidelines

### Colors
- Background: #FAF6F0 (warm beige)
- Card background: #FFFFFF (white)
- Primary accent: #F5C842 (yellow)
- Secondary accent: #E8524A (coral red)
- Success/progress: #4CAF50 (green)
- Text primary: #2D2D2D (dark)
- Text secondary: #9E9E9E (gray)

### Style
- Rounded corners everywhere (border-radius: 16px for cards, 8px for buttons)
- Soft shadows on cards
- Clean minimal layout
- Warm and friendly feel

### Typography
- Font: rounded/friendly style
- Headings: bold, dark
- Body: regular weight, good line height

### Components
- Cards with white background and soft shadow
- Pill-shaped tags and badges
- Progress bars with rounded ends
- Checkbox style task items
- Playful emoji/icon accents
